# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 014
**Title:** Heatstroke
**Slug:** heatstroke
**Category:** Environmental Hazards

## The Death (One Sentence)
A hardcore ultra-marathoner dies of hyperthermia in a hostel with no air conditioning during a record heat wave, his elite athlete's confidence becoming the very thing that kills him.

## Tagline
"His body was a temple. The heat made it a crematorium."

## Themes
1. The cult of fitness and the illusion of physical invincibility
2. Climate change making budget travel increasingly dangerous
3. How athletic identity can blind people to genuine danger

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 35%
- Horror: 40%
- Satire: 25%

## Target Reader
Fitness enthusiasts who recognize the "I can push through anything" mindset, travelers who've suffered through hostel heat, anyone who's watched someone refuse to acknowledge they need help.

## Comparison Titles
- Born to Run meets The Painted Bird (extreme suffering)

## Content Warnings
- Graphic hyperthermia symptoms
- Confusion and delirium
- Seizures
- Death from overheating
- Athletic/body dysmorphia themes
